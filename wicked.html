Wicked (2024)

Genre: Musical, Fantasy

Director: Jon M. Chu

Cast: Cynthia Erivo, Ariana Grande, Jonathan Bailey, Michelle Yeoh

Synopsis: *Wicked* tells the untold story of the witches of Oz, following the unlikely friendship between Elphaba and Glinda. As they navigate their opposing destinies, they uncover the truth behind the Wizard’s rule and the events leading up to *The Wizard of Oz.*

Review: A visually stunning and emotionally powerful adaptation of the beloved Broadway musical. The performances by Cynthia Erivo and Ariana Grande bring new depth to the iconic roles, while the film's spectacular visuals and grand musical numbers capture the magic of Oz in a way never seen before. Fans of the stage show will appreciate the faithful adaptation, while newcomers will be enchanted by the film’s emotional core.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)
