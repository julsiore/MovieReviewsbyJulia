Encanto (2021)

Genre: Animation, Fantasy, Family

Director: Byron Howard, Jared Bush

Cast: Stephanie Beatriz, María Cecilia Botero, John Leguizamo

Synopsis: Deep in the mountains of Colombia, the magical Madrigal family lives in a home that grants each member a unique power—except for Mirabel. When the magic starts to fade, Mirabel embarks on a journey to save her family and discover her true strength.

Review: *Encanto* is a heartwarming and visually stunning film that celebrates family, identity, and self-worth. The music by Lin-Manuel Miranda brings vibrant energy, with songs like *We Don’t Talk About Bruno* becoming instant classics. The story’s emotional depth and cultural richness make it a standout in Disney’s animated lineup.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)
